Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance and support for the software we develop. We work with clients to establish a maintenance plan that fits their needs and ensures their software continues to function smoothly.

How do you ensure the security of my data and information?

We take data security very seriously and use industry-standard practices and protocols to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of our clients' data and information.

How long does it take to complete a project?

The timeline for each project depends on various factors, such as the scope of work, the complexity of the project, and the client's timeline. We work closely with clients to establish a timeline that meets their needs and expectations.

What services does HMZones provide?

Services for software development are offered by HMZones. Our services encompass the creation of websites, mobile applications, custom application software, software maintenance, and support.

The price of the service may depend upon the difficulty of the project, the necessary features and functionalities, the size of the project, and the specific requirements you have.
